about me

I'm an engineer and a designer. My earliest memories are of my dad and I working in the garage, and I've been tinkering ever since. I have experience with woodworking, metalworking, composites, and rapid prototyping. I'm always in the middle of building something.

I'm obsessed with urban design and architecture. I'm interested in the effects the growing urban population and climate change are going to have on our built environment. I want to be a part of designing our cities and transportation systems for the challenges of the 21st century.
